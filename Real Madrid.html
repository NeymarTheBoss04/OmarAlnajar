Real Madrid youth coaches, along with the Euro Soccer team, will be selecting 5-10 players from each of the camps for a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to train for 5 days at Real Madrid’s Campus in Madrid, Spain. They will be given the chance to join an exclusive, invitation-only Real Madrid Camp to be held during the week of Thanksgiving 2022 and/or Spring Break 2023 at Real Madrid City’s state-of-the-art training facilities. Participants will experience what it’s like to be an academy player for the world’s best football club.
